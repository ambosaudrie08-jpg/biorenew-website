Welcome to BioRenew!

BioRenew is a student-led environmental initiative that focuses on turning organic waste into renewable energy. Our goal is to promote sustainable waste management practices while developing practical solutions that benefit both the community and the environment.

Our Mission

To educate and empower communities in adopting innovative, eco-friendly technologies that transform biodegradable waste into useful energy sources.

About Us

BioRenew was founded by students from the Technological University of the Philippines who are passionate about sustainability and environmental engineering. This project was developed as part of our academic research focusing on renewable energy production using organic waste.

Our Vision: A future where waste becomes a valuable source of clean energy.

Our Objectives:

  • Promote sustainable waste management.
  • Develop low-cost renewable energy solutions.
  • Spread environmental awareness through educational outreach.
  • Encourage innovation in green technology.

Our Project

Our research focuses on converting organic waste into renewable energy through anaerobic digestion, producing biogas that can be used for cooking, electricity, or heating. We aim to develop an affordable model that communities can adopt.

Features:

  • Efficient breakdown of organic waste.
  • Biogas generation for energy use.
  • Environment-friendly and low-cost system design.

Technologies Used:

  • Biogas digester system
  • Gas storage and pressure valves
  • Sensors for monitoring temperature and pH
